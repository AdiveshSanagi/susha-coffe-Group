Part of Coffee trade

Susha Coffee brings together the way of growing coffee with the new way of selling it all around the world. They connect the people who grow coffee to the people who buy it. Susha Groups does this by being part of the trade of coffee.
We are based in the Western Ghats of Karnataka and our goal is to help farmers who grow specialty coffee. These specialty coffee farmers are very small-scale. We want to connect them with people all around the world who love specialty coffee. Our specialty coffee is very good.And we think people will really like it. We want to make sure these small specialty coffee farmers can sell their coffee to people in other countries.

We make sure that farmers get a fair price for what they produce and buyers around the world get a steady supply of coffee. This happens because we do not use middlemen. This is very important for farmers and global buyers.
