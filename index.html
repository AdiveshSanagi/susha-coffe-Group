Why Karnataka Coffee is Globally Preferred?

Karnataka is the place where most of India's coffee is grown. In fact, it produces over 70% of the country's coffee. The coffee from Karnataka is really well known world-wide for it's good quality. This is because the coffee plants are grown in the shade in the Western Ghats region, which is a very special place, with a lot of different plants and animals. Karnatakas coffee is special because it is grown this way.

100% Shade-Grown Coffee

Our coffee grows under a kind of forest with two layers of trees. This helps the coffee cherries ripen slowly and gives our coffee a rich aroma. Our coffee gets to mature slowly and that is what makes the aroma of our coffee so complex and nice.

    “The Soul of the Sahyadris in Every Sip”

    Indian Origin. Global Excellence.

    The Sahyadri Hills in Karnataka are part of the Western Ghats, known for dense forests and rich biodiversity. The region has a moderate climate with temperatures around 15°C–28°C and humidity of 60–90%, creating ideal conditions for growing high-quality coffee.
